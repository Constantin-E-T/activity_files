  1. Prepare the vegetables that you want to add to your sandwich
  2. Slice tomatoes and any other veggies that need slicing.
  3. Gather the materials needed on a flat ,clean, and sanitary surface
  4. Take out two slices of your favorite bread.
  5. Open your favorite condiment jar
  6. Insert spreading knife into the jar and load the desired amount of condiment onto knife
  7. Then spread condiment evenly onto one side of a sliced piece of bread.
  8. Choose your favorite meat(s) and place onto one of the pieces of bread on the side with the condiment.
  9. Make sure to spread the meat in a way will go all the way to the edge of the bread.
  10. Place your desired amount of the cheese you have chosen on top of the meat.
  11. Make sure add enough cheese to cover the entire sandwich.
  12. Place the desired toppings (sliced tomatoes, pickles, lettuce, etc.) on top of your cheese
  13. Place the second slice of bread with the condiment covered side down on top of your toppings.
  14. If desired, slice sandwich into a shape that will make it easier to handle. Some common shapes are rectangles where you cut the sandwich down middle or triangles in which you cut the sandwich diagonally through the middle.
  15. Serve and enjoy your sandwich.
